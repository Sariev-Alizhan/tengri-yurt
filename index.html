What is Tengri Yurt?

Tengri Yurt is a handcrafted architectural space rooted in the ancient nomadic traditions of Kazakhstan and reimagined for the modern world. Designed as a circular environment of connection and presence, it brings people together in nature — for retreats, eco-resorts, cultural projects, and transformational gatherings across the globe. More than a structure, a Tengri Yurt is a living space where community forms naturally, ideas flow freely, and meaningful experiences unfold.

Our Craft

FROM TRADITION TO TRANSFORMATION

01

Design & Consultation

Understanding your vision, space requirements, and intended use. Every yurt begins with a conversation about purpose and place.

02

Material Selection

Choosing premium natural materials — sustainable wood from Kazakh forests, hand-pressed felt, and weather-resistant canvas.

03

Frame Construction

Master craftsmen shape and bend each wooden lattice wall by hand, creating the iconic circular structure that has sheltered nomads for centuries.

04

Felt Making

Traditional wool pressing techniques passed through generations. Each layer is carefully compressed to create natural insulation.

05

Assembly & Details

Hand-stitching decorative elements, painting traditional patterns, and ensuring every joint, rope, and cover meets our exacting standards.

06

Global Installation

Our team travels worldwide to install your yurt, ensuring it stands perfectly in its new home — from desert to mountains to coast.
